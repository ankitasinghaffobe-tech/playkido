About Play Kido

Inspiring young minds since 2006

Our Story

18 Years of Nurturing Excellence

Play Kido was founded in 2006 by Dr. Meena Kapoor with a simple but powerful vision: to create a school where every child feels safe, loved, and inspired to learn. What began as a single nursery classroom with 12 students has grown into a thriving institution serving 1,200+ students.

Today, Play Kido is recognised as one of the leading early childhood education institutions in the region, celebrated for its innovative pedagogy, caring environment, and exceptional student outcomes.

Our Mission

To provide a joyful, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment where every child discovers their unique strengths and develops a lifelong love for learning.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative early education institution that shapes confident, compassionate, and curious global citizens of tomorrow.

Our Values

Respect, Curiosity, Creativity, Integrity, Inclusivity, and Excellence guide everything we do — from lesson planning to playground culture.

Dr. Meena Kapoor
Founder & Principal
Ph.D. Early Childhood Education
Principal's Message

"At Play Kido, we believe that the early years of a child's education are the most formative. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to create an environment where curiosity is celebrated, mistakes are welcomed as learning opportunities, and every child is empowered to dream big. Our Programs

Thoughtfully designed learning journeys for every stage of early childhood.

Ages 2–3 Years

Nursery Program

Our Nursery program is a warm, loving space for our youngest learners. Through sensory play, music, movement, and stories, children develop fine motor skills, social confidence, and early communication abilities. Each day is carefully structured yet flexible enough to follow each child's natural curiosity.

  • Sensory Play Stations
  • Music and Movement Classes
  • Story Time and Language Development
  • Art and Craft Exploration
Ages 3–4 Years

Pre-Kindergarten

Pre-KG bridges home and school life. Children develop their vocabulary, counting skills, and emotional intelligence in a structured, nurturing environment. Through guided discovery and collaborative play, they build the foundation for formal schooling.

  • Phonics Introduction
  • Number Sense Activities
  • Social Skills and Sharing
  • Creative Expression
Ages 4–6 Years

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten program accelerates academic readiness through a play-based yet rigorous curriculum. Children learn to read simple texts, solve basic math problems, conduct science explorations, and express themselves through art and performance.

  • Reading and Writing
  • Early Mathematics
  • Science Experiments
  • Computer Literacy Basics
Ages 6–11 Years

Primary Education (Grades 1–5)

Primary school at Play Kido combines academic excellence with holistic development. Students explore English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, Hindi, and an elective language, alongside Art, Physical Education, and Music.

  • CBSE-Aligned Curriculum
  • Project-Based Learning
  • STEM Integration
  • Leadership and Values Education
